Discover
The Heart Of Bintulu

Location

Red Hotel Bintulu is strategically located in the vibrant town of Bintulu, Sarawak, making it the perfect base for both business and leisure travelers. Our prime location offers easy access to major attractions, business centers, shopping areas, and cultural sites, ensuring a convenient and enriching stay for all our guests.

Nearby Attractions

1
Tanjung Batu Beach

Enjoy a relaxing day at Tanjung Batu Beach, a popular spot for both locals and tourists. Take a stroll along the sandy shores, have a picnic, or simply soak up the sun while enjoying the scenic coastal views.

2
Taman Tumbina Bintulu

Visit Taman Tumbina, a unique zoo and botanical garden. Home to a variety of wildlife and plant species, this family-friendly destination offers a great opportunity to explore nature and learn about local flora and fauna.

3
Bintulu Promenade

Take a leisurely walk along the Bintulu Promenade. This waterfront area offers beautiful views, a cool breeze, and a range of dining options. It’s an ideal spot for an evening outing.

4
Borneo International Kite Festival

If you’re visiting during the Borneo International Kite Festival, make sure to attend this colorful and lively event. Watch stunning kite displays from around the world and enjoy the festive atmosphere.

5
Similajau National Park

For nature lovers, a visit to Similajau National Park is a must. Explore the park’s diverse ecosystems, go for a jungle trek, or take a boat trip to spot dolphins and other marine life. The park is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic landscapes.

Shopping and Dining

1
The Spring Bintulu

A modern shopping mall offering a wide range of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities.

2
Pasar Utama and Pasar Tamu Bintulu

Experience local culture and cuisine at these bustling markets, perfect for sampling local delicacies and buying unique souvenirs.

Transportation

1
Bintulu Airport (BTU)

Only a 20-minute drive from the hotel, making travel convenient for our guests.

2
Public Transportation

Easy access to local taxis and public buses for exploring the town and beyond.

3
Free Parking

Complimentary parking available for guests traveling by car.

Cultural Sites

1
Tua Pek Kong Temple

One of Bintulu's oldest and most significant Chinese temples, offering a glimpse into the local culture and history.

2
Bintulu History and Cultural Corridor

Learn about Bintulu's rich history and heritage through interactive exhibits and displays.
