Things To Do

Red Hotel Bintulu is perfectly situated to give you easy access to a variety of attractions and activities in and around the vibrant town of Bintulu. Whether you’re an adventure seeker, a history buff, or just looking to relax, there’s something for everyone.

Nearby Attractions

1
Tanjung Batu Beach

Enjoy a relaxing day at Tanjung Batu Beach, a popular spot for both locals and tourists. Take a stroll along the sandy shores, have a picnic, or simply soak up the sun while enjoying the scenic coastal views.

2
Taman Tumbina Bintulu

Visit Taman Tumbina, a unique zoo and botanical garden. Home to a variety of wildlife and plant species, this family-friendly destination offers a great opportunity to explore nature and learn about local flora and fauna.

3
Bintulu Promenade

Take a leisurely walk along the Bintulu Promenade. This waterfront area offers beautiful views, a cool breeze, and a range of dining options. It’s an ideal spot for an evening outing.

4
Borneo International Kite Festival

If you’re visiting during the Borneo International Kite Festival, make sure to attend this colorful and lively event. Watch stunning kite displays from around the world and enjoy the festive atmosphere.

5
Similajau National Park

For nature lovers, a visit to Similajau National Park is a must. Explore the park’s diverse ecosystems, go for a jungle trek, or take a boat trip to spot dolphins and other marine life. The park is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic landscapes.

Cultural Experiences

1
Pasar Utama and Pasar Tamu Bintulu

Experience the local culture at Pasar Utama and Pasar Tamu, the main markets in Bintulu. Browse through stalls selling fresh produce, local delicacies, and handmade crafts. It’s a great place to immerse yourself in the local way of life.

2
Tua Pek Kong Temple

Visit the Tua Pek Kong Temple, one of the oldest and most important Chinese temples in Bintulu. The temple’s intricate architecture and peaceful ambiance make it a worthwhile stop.

3
Bintulu History and Cultural Corridor

Explore the Bintulu History and Cultural Corridor to learn about the town’s history and cultural heritage. The exhibits and displays provide insights into the local traditions and historical milestones of Bintulu.

Outdoor Activities

1
Golfing at Bintulu Golf Club

Enjoy a round of golf at the Bintulu Golf Club. This well-maintained course offers a challenging game with beautiful views, perfect for both amateur and professional golfers.

2
Watersports

Try your hand at various watersports such as jet-skiing, kayaking, or windsurfing. The coastal areas around Bintulu provide excellent conditions for these exciting activities.

3
Fishing Trips

Join a fishing trip to experience the thrill of catching fish in the rich waters of Bintulu. Local operators offer guided trips that cater to both beginners and experienced anglers.

4
Borneo International Kite Festival

If you’re visiting during the Borneo International Kite Festival, make sure to attend this colorful and lively event. Watch stunning kite displays from around the world and enjoy the festive atmosphere.

5
Similajau National Park

For nature lovers, a visit to Similajau National Park is a must. Explore the park’s diverse ecosystems, go for a jungle trek, or take a boat trip to spot dolphins and other marine life. The park is known for its rich biodiversity and scenic landscapes.

Relaxation and Leisure

1
Spa and Wellness

Unwind with a relaxing spa treatment. Red Hotel Bintulu offers a range of spa services to rejuvenate your mind and body after a day of exploring.

2
Shopping at The Spring Bintulu

Spend some time shopping at The Spring Bintulu, a modern shopping mall with a variety of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities.

Evening Entertainment

1
Local Nightlife

Discover Bintulu’s nightlife by visiting local bars and pubs. Enjoy live music, local drinks, and a lively atmosphere.

2
Cinemas and Entertainment Centers

Catch the latest movies at local cinemas or visit entertainment centers for activities like bowling and arcade games.
