About Us
Welcome to Red Hotel Bintulu

Nestled in the heart of Bintulu, Red Hotel Bintulu offers a perfect blend of comfort, convenience, and contemporary style. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, our hotel provides a welcoming retreat with modern amenities and exceptional service.

Our Story

Red Hotel Bintulu was established with a vision to provide a luxurious yet affordable lodging option in the bustling town of Bintulu. Our commitment to hospitality excellence and attention to detail ensures that every guest enjoys a memorable stay.

Location

Conveniently located near major attractions, business districts, and transportation hubs, Red Hotel Bintulu is your ideal base for exploring the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of the area. Discover nearby points of interest such as the Taman Tumbina Zoo, Tanjung Batu Beach, and the iconic Bintulu Promenade.
